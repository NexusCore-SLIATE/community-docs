## 📐 Exporting Guidelines for Designers
When handing off designs to developers within the `software-engineering` track:
1. **Always use vector formats:** Export structural icons, graphics, and logos strictly as clean `.svg` files to prevent rendering pixelation.
2. **Clean Group Names:** Ensure Figma component layer names match standard web naming conventions before passing them over to developers for coding.
